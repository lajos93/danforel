Welcome to Danforel!

Smoked trout fillets, nutritious everyday food!

A leading company of Europe

Danforel was established more than 60 years ago on the basis of the consideration that efficient sales and exports require rational production and distribution. Today, Danforel is a privately-owned limited company with approximately 120 employees. Danforel occupies a position as Europe's leading and biggest producer of smoked trout fillet products.
